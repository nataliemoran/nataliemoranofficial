Artist Bio

Natalie Moran is an exciting growing independent pop act. Natalie releases sensational tunes that are snappy, energetic, fierce, fuelled with a balance of sweet melodic intimate singles. "Her Tunes are ready to hit air waves" (HotPress).

A flourishing singer and songwriter, recognition continues to reach new heights. Natalie’s tracks have gained notoriety with live performances and features across publications and radio stations. Vocally distinctive with tones capturing delightful highs and powerful punches her tracks cross the genres of electropop, acoustic pop and pop soul.
